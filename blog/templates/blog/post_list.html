Free Open Source Photogrammetry Foundation (FOSPHO)

 

Goals

The objective of our foundation is to promote the development, diffusion and the use of free open source photogrammetric/computer vision tools and knowledge, including e.g. :

  • free open source software under the CECILL, GNU or equivalent licences;
  • cours materials (e.g. books, videos, www) under the Creative Commons license or equivalent;
  • hardware (IT, cameras, etc.) under the GNU, TARP licenses or equivalent.
